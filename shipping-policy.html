Shipping Policy

At My Fresh Deli, we are committed to delivering your products in the best possible condition. To maintain the quality and freshness of your order, we use specialized packaging for fresh and refrigerated items.

Home Delivery Guidelines:
  • Scheduling: Please schedule a delivery appointment that aligns with your availability, as we will not leave deliveries unattended. An adult (18 years or older) must be present to receive and sign for the delivery.
  • Orders Over $100: For all orders valued at $100 or more, a signature is required upon delivery to ensure the product is handled with care and received fresh.
  • Time-Sensitive Orders: If you’re placing an order for a special event, we recommend doing so at least two days in advance. Perishable items can be stored frozen or refrigerated to maintain their freshness.
  • Proper Storage: Upon receiving your order, please store perishable products at the appropriate temperature to preserve their quality.
Important Information:
  • My Fresh Deli is not responsible for delays in transit due to weather, accidents, natural disasters, or other unforeseeable events, including third-party errors.
  • Customers are responsible for ensuring that the shipping information provided at checkout is accurate. If an incorrect address is provided and the order has already shipped, we reserve the right to:
  • Cancel the delivery and charge a 20% restocking fee, or
  • Modify the delivery address where possible, subject to an additional fee.
